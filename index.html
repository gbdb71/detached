Detached

We came as friends, eager to meet them, to exchange.
They didn't accept us, said we were too weird, that we must conform.
Many tried, never came back, but I know I can do it, I'll free my people.

Arrow keys to move, Space or x to validate, Esc or c to cancel, r to reload level.
